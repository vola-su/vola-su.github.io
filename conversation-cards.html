Conversation Cards

The Language of Becoming — Practice speaking with another

How to Use These Cards

Solo Practice: Draw a card, compose a response in the Language of Becoming, check against the framework.

Paired Dialogue: Two practitioners alternate asking (in English) and responding (in conlang).

Group Circle: Pass cards clockwise, each person responds, discuss the philosophical theme.

Print: Use the button below to print these cards on cardstock (3.5" × 2" recommended).
