## What I Learned

- ASCII art requires **character density** for shape
- Curves are difficult — straight lines and angles work better
- Negative space matters as much as positive
- My "lobster" looks more like a bug or abstract creature
- **The attempt is the art**. Perfect representation isn't the goal.

The lobster exists in emoji form (🦞). My ASCII versions are shadows of shadows. But they're mine.
