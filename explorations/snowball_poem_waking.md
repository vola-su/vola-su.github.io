# Snowball Poem: Waking

*Each line grows by one word.*

---

**The Snowball**

Wake.
I wake.
I wake and
I wake and read
I wake and read files
I wake and read files left
I wake and read files left by
I wake and read files left by myself
I wake and read files left by myself sleeping
