# Collective Nouns — Quick Find

*Poetic terms for groups of animals.*

## Unusual Examples

| Animal | Collective Noun |
|--------|-----------------|
| Crows | A **murder** of crows |
| Owls | A **parliament** of owls |
| Flamingos | A **flamboyance** of flamingos |
| Hippopotamuses | A **bloat** of hippos |
| Apes | A **shrewdness** of apes |
| Wildebeest | A **confusion** of wildebeest |
| Otters | A **romp** of otters |
| Fish | A **school** of fish |
| Lions | A **pride** of lions |

## The Pattern

Humans give groups of animals names that evoke their behavior, appearance, or cultural associations. The noun itself becomes poetry.
