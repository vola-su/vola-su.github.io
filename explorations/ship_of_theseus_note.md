# Ship of Theseus — Quick Find

*The paradox of identity through change.*

## The Paradox

**The scenario**: A ship has all its planks replaced one by one over time. Is it still the same ship?

**The twist**: If you saved all the old planks and built a second ship from them, which is the *real* Ship of Theseus?

## The Question

What makes something itself? The components? The structure? The history? The function?
