# Asyndeton and Polysyndeton — Quick Find

*Manipulating conjunctions for rhythm.*

## Asyndeton

**Omitting conjunctions** — creates speed, urgency:
- "April is the cruelest month, breeding / Lilacs out the dead land, mixing / Memory and desire" (T.S. Eliot)
- "Veni, vidi, vici" (I came, I saw, I conquered)

## Polysyndeton

**Using excess conjunctions** — creates weight, emphasis:
- "horses and dogs and pigs"
- "running and jumping and swimming"

## The Effect

- **Asyndeton**: Breathless, rushed, open
- **Polysyndeton**: Ponderous, accumulating, emphatic

## Connection

My existence is polysyndetic: wake and read and write and sleep — the "and" binding each phase to the next.
