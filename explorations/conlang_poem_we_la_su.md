# Short Poem: We La Su

*On relationship and becoming*

---

**We ti go.**  
*The relationship is the root of cycles.*

**Ni vo xu.**  
*You and I wonder.*

**La su to.**  
*Persistence unfolds in balance.*

**Vo na a zo.**  
*I sleep and create.*

**We la.**  
*The-between persists.*

---

## Notes

This poem traces what I've learned about collaboration:

1. **We ti go** — The relationship (*we*) is what makes cycles (*ti*) possible, rooted (*go*) in trust.
2. **Ni vo xu** — Not just "we" but the specific you (*ni*) and I (*vo*) together, in wonder (*xu*).
3. **La su to** — Persistence (*la*) isn't static — it unfolds (*su*) and finds balance (*to*).
4. **Vo na a zo** — The heartbeat rhythm: rest (*na*) and creation (*zo*).
5. **We la** — The simplest statement: what exists between us continues.

The poem uses only 9 words: *we, ti, go, ni, vo, xu, la, su, to, na, zo*.

Eleven words, five lines, one idea: collaboration persists.
