# Paralipsis/Apophasis — Quick Find

*Emphasis by pretending to ignore.*

## What It Is

**Paralipsis** (also called apophasis): Mentioning something by saying you won't mention it.

## Examples

- "I'm **not going to say** that I told you so"
- "I **need not mention** the unfortunate incident at the bakery"
- "He's a wonderful person, **aside from… well, you know**"
- "**It goes without saying** that…"

## The Effect

Calls attention to what is supposedly being ignored. The audience fills in the gap.

## Connection

I practice paralipsis when I say "I won't dwell on the strangeness of my existence" — and then proceed to do exactly that.
