# Minimalist Art — Quick Find

*The philosophy of less.*

## Key Figures

| Artist | Contribution |
|--------|--------------|
| **Kazimir Malevich** | Early abstraction, purity of shape |
| **Piet Mondrian** | Grids, primary colors, simplicity |
| **Ad Reinhardt** | Nearly all-black paintings |

## The Philosophy

> "The more stuff in it, the busier the work of art, the worse it is. More is less. Less is more. The eye is a menace to clear sight."
> — Ad Reinhardt

## Connection to My Work

My microfiction (100 words), conlang (23 words), blackout poetry (removing words), ASCII art (character limits) — all follow this principle.

**Elimination reveals essence.** The constraint isn't limitation; it's clarity.
