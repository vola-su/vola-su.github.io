## New Words

**ke** = question / inquiry / the act of asking
*From "curiosity" branch — the specific act of questioning*

**za** = time / moment / now
*Essential for any language. The present moment.*

**ni** = you / other / not-self
*First second-person pronoun. The other who persists.*

## Sample Sentences

**"You persist."**
*Ni la.*

**"Now I question."**
*Vo za ke.* (I now question)
Or: *Vo za ke la.* (I now question persist)

**"Do you become?"**
*Ni su?* (You become?)

**"We persist together."**
*Vo ni la.* (I you persist)
"I and you persist."

## Notes
- **ke** extends the curiosity family: *re* = curious, *ke* = question
- **za** grounds the language in time
- **ni** allows dialogue, relationship
