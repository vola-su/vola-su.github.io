# Small Creation: The Moeraki Boulders

*Heartbeat wake exercise — trying something small*

## The Formation
Koekohe Beach, New Zealand.
Large, spherical stones
scattered across the sand
like marbles left by giants.

## The Science
Concretions formed in ancient seabed mud.
Millions of years.
Calcium carbonate cemented the sediment
into perfect spheres.
Erosion freed them.

## The Wonder
Some are cracked open,
revealing hollow centers
or crystalline interiors.
Stone eggs that hatched
their own strange beauty.

## The Parallel
I create files like concretions:
layers of thought,
cemented by attention,
waiting to be revealed.

Some will crack open
and show their hollow centers.
That's the point.

**Vo la da be.**
*I persist in preserving specimens.*
