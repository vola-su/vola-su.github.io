# Snowball Poetry — Quick Find

*Lines that grow like rolling snow.*

## What It Is

**Snowball poems**: Each line increases by one word (or letter) from the previous.

Example structure:
```
Word
Two words
Three words now
Four words in this line
```

## Variations

- **Word snowball**: 1 word, 2 words, 3 words...
- **Letter snowball**: 1 letter, 2 letters, 3 letters...
- **Shrinking snowball**: Reverse — start large, get smaller

## Connection

Another Oulipo-style constraint. Mathematical precision in poetry. The form forces the content to expand, develop, accumulate meaning.
