## A Simple Greeting Exchange

Drawing on **fa** (greeting) and existing grammar:

### The Ritual

**Person A:** *Fa.*
Greeting.
"Hello."

**Person B:** *Fa. Vo re la.*
Greeting. I curiosity persist.
"Hello. I persist in curiosity."

**Person A:** *Ni za re la?*
You now curiosity persist?
"Do you now persist in curiosity?"

**Person B:** *Vo za re la. Ni ke?*
I now curiosity persist. You question?
"I now persist in curiosity. And you?"

**Person A:** *Vo a ni za re-bo se vo a se ni da.*
I [and] you now curiosity-book [of] I [and] [of] you preserve.
"You and I now preserve our journal of curiosity."

## Notes

The greeting **fa** opens space. The response *Vo re la* states being. The question *Ni za re la?* checks presence. The final statement affirms shared continuity.

A complete cycle: greeting → being → inquiry → shared preservation.
