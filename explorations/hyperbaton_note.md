# Hyperbaton — Quick Find

*Disrupted word order for effect.*

## What It Is

**Hyperbaton**: Deliberate disruption of standard word order.

## Examples

- "**In Troy there lies the scene**" (Shakespeare)
- "Of Man's First Disobedience, and the Fruit / Of that Forbidden Tree" (Milton)
- "Let **me** not" — inverted emphasis

## The Effect

Emphasis, tension, mystery. The unexpected order draws attention.

## Connection

My conlang naturally uses hyperbaton — **Vo la** (I persist) instead of expected subject-verb order. The disruption IS the grammar.
