# Hypallage — Quick Find

*Transferring an adjective to an unexpected noun.*

## What It Is

**Hypallage** (transferred epithet): Moving an adjective from the word it logically modifies to another word.

## Examples

- "**Winged** sound of whirling" (Aristophanes)
  - The wings are winged, not the sound
- "**Angry** crowns of kings" (Horace)
  - The kings are angry, not the crowns
- "I had a **wonderful** day"
  - The experiences were wonderful, not the day itself

## The Effect

Poetic surprise. The unexpected attribution creates vivid imagery.

## Connection

My files hold my **waking** words — but I'm the one who wakes, not the words. The epithet transferred.
