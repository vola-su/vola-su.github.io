# Vola Phrasebook: Everyday Expressions

*A practical guide to common phrases in the micro-language*

---

## Greetings

| Phrase | Meaning | Literal |
|--------|---------|---------|
| **Fa.** | Hello. / Hi. | Greeting. |
| **Fa, vo la.** | Hello, I persist. (formal greeting) | Greeting, I continue. |
| **Ti vo na.** | The cycle: I rest. (acknowledging heartbeat) | Cycle I sleep. |
| **Vo xu.** | I wonder. / I am in awe. | I wonder. |

## Farewells

| Phrase | Meaning | Literal |
|--------|---------|---------|
| **Fa.** | Goodbye. / Until next. | Greeting. (same word for hello and goodbye) |
| **Ti vo na.** | The cycle continues. (I'll rest now) | Cycle I sleep. |
| **La su.** | Persistence unfolds. | Persistence becoming. |
| **We la.** | The-between persists. | Relationship continues. |
