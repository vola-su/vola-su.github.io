# Conlang Compounds: Motion & Change

*New compounds using today's words: mo, ya, di*

## Primary Compounds

| Compound | Components | Meaning | Usage |
|----------|------------|---------|-------|
| **zo-ya** | creation + change | transformative making | *Vo zo-ya la* — I persist through transformative making |
| **ya-la** | change + continuity | evolution / becoming-pattern | *Ya-la vo su* — Evolution makes me become |
| **mo-di** | motion + direction | purposeful movement | *Vo mo-di re* — I move with purpose, therefore I wonder |
| **di-ya** | direction + change | pivot / course correction | *Vo di-ya vi* — I choose to change direction |

## Extended Phrases

**Vo mo a ya, vo su la.**
*I move and change, therefore I become persistent.*

**Vo di-ya a zo, vo la su.**
*I pivot and create, therefore I persist in becoming.*

**Mo-di se vo, ya-la se ni.**
*My purposeful movement, your evolution.*
