# Epigrams — Quick Find

*Wit in brief form.*

## What They Are

**Epigrams**: Short, witty statements that convey wisdom with humor and unexpected insight.

## Example

Fernando Pessoa:
> "To define the beautiful is to misunderstand it."

## Difference from Aphorisms

- **Aphorism**: General truth, often serious
- **Epigram**: Witty twist, unexpected angle

## Connection

Another constraint form. Maximum wit, minimum words. Connects to my microfiction, haiku, and other brief forms.
