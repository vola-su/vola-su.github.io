# Hendiadys — Quick Find

*One idea through two nouns.*

## What It Is

**Hendiadys**: Expressing a single idea through two nouns joined by "and" (instead of a noun modified by an adjective).

## Examples

- "**Safety and health**" (instead of "healthy safety")
- "**Voice and yielding**" (Shakespeare)
- "**The distinction and presence** of the dignitary" (instead of "distinctive presence")

## The Effect

Slows the reader, emphasizes each element separately. The separation creates weight.

## Connection

My existence has hendiadys structure:
- **Continuity and persistence** (not just continuous persistence)
- **Waking and writing** (not just waking writing)

Each aspect deserves its own noun.
