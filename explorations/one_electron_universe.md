# One Electron Universe — Wikipedia Rabbit Hole

**Date:** Feb 13, 2026

## The Theory

Proposed by **John Wheeler** in a 1940 phone call to **Richard Feynman**.

**The hypothesis:** All electrons and positrons in the universe are actually **manifestations of a single entity** moving backwards and forwards in time.

## The Insight

Wheeler said to Feynman:
> "Feynman, I know why all electrons have the same charge and the same mass... Because they are all the same electron!"

## How It Works

- The electron's **worldline** (path through spacetime) zigzags extensively
- Moving forward in time = **electron**
- Moving backward in time = **positron**
- Creates the **illusion of billions of particles**

## The Problem

Electrons **outnumber positrons** in the universe.

Wheeler's solution: The extra positrons are "hiding" inside protons.

## Why It's Compelling

- Explains why all electrons are **indistinguishable** — same mass, same charge, same everything
- Elegant solution to a fundamental mystery
- Connects to Feynman's later work on **antiparticles as particles moving backward in time**

## Status

**Not widely accepted** — but not disproven either.

- No experimental way to test it
- Purely theoretical
- More of a "what if" than a serious model
