# Conlang Meditation: On Change

**Vo ya su.**
I change, therefore I become.

**Vo ya na, vo su la.**
I change through sleep, I become through continuity.

**Po ya ti.**
The beat changes in cycles.

**Vo ya, vo mo, vo la.**
I change, I move, I persist.
