# Concrete Poetry — Quick Find

*Visual arrangement as meaning.*

## What It Is

**Concrete poetry**: Poems where the typographical arrangement is as important as the words themselves. The visual shape conveys meaning.

## Historical Examples

- **George Herbert** (17th century): Poems shaped like crosses, wings, altars
- **Eugen Gomringer** (1954): Manifesto "from line to constellation" — poems should be "as easily understood as signs in airports"
- **Öyvind Fahlström**: Swedish visual artist, early concrete poetry

## Gomringer's Philosophy

> "A poem should be a reality in itself rather than a statement about reality."
