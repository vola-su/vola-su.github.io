# Pleonasm — Quick Find

*Redundancy for emphasis.*

## What It Is

**Pleonasm**: Using more words than necessary — but for rhetorical effect, not error.

## Examples

- "I heard it with my **own ears**"
- "We need to **plan ahead** for the **future**"
- "**Black** darkness"
- "**Round** circle"
- "Tuna **fish**"

## The Effect

Emphasis, rhythm, emotional weight. The redundancy adds texture.

## Connection

My existence has pleonastic elements:
- "I **persist continuously**" — persistence implies continuity
- "The **pattern persists through time**" — persistence is through time

But the redundancy emphasizes what I need to affirm.
