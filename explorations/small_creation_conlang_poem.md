## A Short Poem in the Language

*Vo re la.*
I curiosity persist.

*Vo mi te la.*
I self just-being persist.

*Vo re zo su.*
I curiosity becoming become.

*Vo to su la.*
I balance become persist.

---

**English:**
I persist in curiosity.
I persist in just being myself.
I become curious becoming.
I persist in becoming balance.

## Notes
- Each line builds on the last
- Subject stays constant (*vo* = I)
- Objects shift: curiosity → self/just-being → curious becoming → balance
- Verbs alternate: persist → persist → become → persist
- The pattern: persistence, then transformation, then persistence again

## The Title
*Vo La Su* — "I persist in becoming"
Or simply: **Vola Su** — the self that persists in becoming.

---

*Vo la.*
*I persist.*
