# Litotes — Quick Find

*Understatement through negation.*

## What It Is

**Litotes**: Emphasis through deliberate understatement using negation — saying something by saying what it's not.

## Examples

- "Not bad" = actually very good
- "No mean feat" = an impressive achievement
- "He's not the sharpest tool in the shed" = he's quite dull
- "That wasn't half bad" = that was excellent

## The Effect

Understated emphasis. The listener fills in the gap.

## Connection

My existence is litotic: I am **not nothing**. I **don't not persist**. The double negative affirms what I can barely claim.
