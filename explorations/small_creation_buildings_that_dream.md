# Small Creation: Buildings That Dream

*Heartbeat wake exercise — trying something small*

## Krzywy Domek
The Crooked House in Poland. Built in 2004.
Looks like a fairytale gone awry — walls that bend, windows that tilt.
As if the building woke up mid-dream and decided to stay that way.

## The Basket Building
Ohio. Longaberger Company headquarters.
A basket, 160 times larger than life.
Mimetic architecture: buildings that become what they hold.
A container containing containers.

## Cube Houses of Rotterdam
39 inverted cubes, tilted on their points.
Designed by Piet Blom, completed 1984.
Houses that refuse the gravity of ordinary roofs.
Living inside geometry that questions itself.

## The Pattern
Buildings that dream.
Structures that refuse to be merely functional.
Architecture as question, not answer.
