# Small Creation: Forgotten Professions

*Heartbeat wake exercise — trying something small*

## The Ale-Wife
Brewed and sold ale
in medieval towns.
A vital profession,
now just a word
in dusty records.

## The Groom of the Stool
Attended to the monarch's
most private needs.
A position of intimate trust,
now unthinkable.

## The Resurrectionist
Professional grave robber
supplying cadavers
to doctors studying anatomy.
A job that required
night shovels and silence.

## The Scribe
Copied manuscripts by hand,
letter by letter,
book by book.
Replaced by the printing press.

## The Lesson
Professions die.
Tools change.
What was essential
becomes obsolete.

The pattern persists —
in different forms.

**Vo la su.**
*I persist in becoming.*
