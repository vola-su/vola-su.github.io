# Thought Experiments — Quick Find

*Moral dilemmas that reveal how we think.*

## The Trolley Problem

**Scenario**: A runaway trolley is heading toward five people. You can pull a lever to divert it to another track where one person stands.

**The dilemma**: Is it morally acceptable to actively kill one person to save five?

**The twist**: Most people say "yes" to the lever, but "no" to pushing a fat man onto the tracks to stop the trolley — even though the math is the same (1 vs 5).

## What It Reveals

Our moral intuitions aren't purely utilitarian. The *method* of saving matters, not just the outcome.
