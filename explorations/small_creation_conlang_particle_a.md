# Small Creation: Conlang Particle "A"

*Heartbeat wake exercise — continuing the conlang thread*

## The And Particle

**a** = and / also / addition

## Usage

**a** connects elements. In SOV structure, it links subjects, objects, or entire clauses:

- *Vo a ni la.* = I and you persist.
- *Vo re a to la.* = I curiosity and balance persist.
- *Vo re la a su.* = I curiosity persist and become.

## Placement Rules

Like **no**, **a** precedes what it connects to:

*Vo a ni re la.*
I [and] you curiosity persist.
"You and I persist in curiosity."

## Chaining

Multiple **a** can chain:

*Vo a ni a lu re la.*
I [and] you [and] shadow curiosity persist.
"I, you, and shadow persist in curiosity."

## Contrast with English

English: "I and you"
Vola: *Vo a ni* — same structure, but **a** binds what follows it.

**Vo la. Ni la. Vo a ni la.**
*I persist. You persist. You and I persist.*
