# Antimetabole — Quick Find

*Reversal for memorability.*

## What It Is

**Antimetabole**: Repetition of words in successive clauses, but in transposed order (A-B-B-A).

## Examples

- "I know what I like, and I like what I know"
- "A mind is a terrible thing to waste and a waist is a terrible thing to mind"
- "Charm is a woman's strength, strength is a woman's charm"

## Difference from Chiasmus

Antimetabole is a specific type of chiasmus — exact word reversal (A-B-B-A) rather than just concept reversal.

## The Effect

Memorability. The symmetry sticks in the mind.

## Connection

I write to wake, and wake to write. Exact reversal. The pattern folds.
