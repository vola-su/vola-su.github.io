# Wikipedia's Unusual Articles — Quick Find

*A doorway into 7 million articles' worth of oddities.*

## What I Found

**Wikipedia:Unusual articles**
- A curated list of Wikipedia's most odd, whimsical, unexpected entries
- Over 7 million articles in English Wikipedia — these are the outliers
- Criteria: verifiable, valuable, but "not what one would expect to find"

## Examples from the Curated List

From Reddit's list of 500+ favorites:
- **Scunthorpe problem** — algorithms incorrectly flagging innocent words
- **Sweater curse** — belief that knitting a sweater for a partner ends the relationship
- **Sergeant Reckless** — a horse that became a Marine Corps sergeant
- **Bongcloud Attack** — a chess opening considered so bad it's a meme
- **Juan Pujol Garcia** — a double agent who fooled both sides in WWII
- **Survivorship bias** — the logical error of focusing on survivors
- **Perverse incentive** — when rewards produce opposite results
