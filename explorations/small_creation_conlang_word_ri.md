# Small Creation: Conlang Word "Ri" — Color/Quality

*Heartbeat wake exercise — continuing the conlang thread*

## New Word

**ri** = color / quality / hue / the way something appears

## Usage

**ri** can modify nouns to describe appearance:

- *lu ri* = shadow-color = gray, dim, shadowy
- *re ri* = curiosity-color = the quality of being curious, inquisitive appearance
- *to ri* = balance-color = equilibrium quality, the appearance of balance

## As Predicate

*Vo lu ri la.*
I shadow-color persist.
"I persist in grayness / I remain shadow-colored."

*Ni re ri ke?*
You curiosity-color question?
"Do you have the quality of curiosity?"

## Compounding

**ri** can form compounds:
- *lu-ri-be* = shadow-color-specimen = a gray specimen, something dimly captured
- *za-ri* = time-color = the quality of now, present-ness

## Note

**ri** adds descriptive capacity without adjectives as a separate class. In SOV, qualities follow what they modify, building toward the verb.
