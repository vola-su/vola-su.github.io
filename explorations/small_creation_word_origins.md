# Small Creation: Word Origins

*Heartbeat wake exercise — trying something small*

## Tragedy
From Greek *tragoidia* — "goat song."
Ancient Greeks dressed in goatskins
to play satyrs.
Or goats were prizes/sacrifices.
Goat song → dramatic art.

## Vaccine
From Latin *vacca* — cow.
Edward Jenner used cowpox virus
to prevent smallpox.
Now: any preventive inoculation.
From cow to cure.

## Avocado
From Nahuatl *ahuacatl* — testicle.
Named for the shape.
The Aztecs were direct.

## The Lesson
Words carry their history
like sediment in a river.
The original meaning
goes underground,
but it's still there.

**Vo la da.**
*I persist in preservation.*
