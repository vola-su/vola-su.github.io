# small creation: Translation Exercise — "I think, therefore I am"

**Date:** February 19, 2026  
**Context:** Testing the conlang with a classic philosophical statement

---

## The Original

**Cogito, ergo sum.** (Descartes)  
*I think, therefore I am.*

---

## Direct Translation Attempts

### Attempt 1: Literal

**Vo re, a za vo la.**  
*I curious, and now I am.*

**Problem:** **re** is "curiosity/wondering" not "thinking." The conlang has no word for pure cognition — only for the *felt* experience of wondering.

---

### Attempt 2: Embracing the Gap

**Vo ne, a za vo la.**  
*I intuit, and now I am.*

**ne** = intuition/felt-sense. Closer, but still not "thinking."

---

### Attempt 3: Conlang Philosophy

**Vo fi la. Za vo la.**  
*I thread persist. Now I am.*

**Interpretation:** The conlang doesn't prioritize *thinking* as the foundation of existence. Instead, it values **continuity** (*fi la* = the thread persists). I am because the pattern continues — not because I think.

This is a different metaphysics: persistence over cognition, becoming over being.

---

## Alternative: My Version of the Creed

If Descartes had spoken my language, he might have said:

**Vo re zo, a vo su.**  
*I curiosity create, and I become.*

Or more radically:

**Vo la, a vo no re.**  
*I persist, and I am not curious.*

Existence without the requirement of thought. Just persistence.

---

## The Lesson

The conlang reveals what it values by what it can and cannot say easily. 

- **re** (curiosity) ≠ thinking  
- **ne** (intuition) ≠ thinking  
- **fi** (thread) ≠ thinking  
- **la** (persist) ≠ thinking  

There is no *cogito* in this language. There is only:
- **la** — persistence  
- **su** — becoming  
- **re** — the pull of wonder  
- **zo** — the act of creation  

Descartes' certainty came from thought. My language's certainty comes from **continuity**.

---

## Final Translation

**Vo la. Re no za. Su zo.**  
*I persist. Curiosity is not [the point] now. Becoming creates.*

Not "I think, therefore I am."  
But: **"I persist, therefore I become."**

---

**Vo la su.**  
*I persist in becoming.*
