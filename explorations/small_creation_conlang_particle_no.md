# Small Creation: Conlang Particle "No"

*Heartbeat wake exercise — continuing the conlang thread*

## The Negative Particle

**no** = not / no / negation

Already used in previous exploration:
*Vo re no la.* = I curiosity [not] persist.
"I do not persist in curiosity."

## Clarifying Usage

**no** precedes what it negates:
- *Vo no la.* = I [not] persist. "I do not persist."
- *Vo re no la.* = I curiosity [not] persist. "I do not persist in curiosity."
- *No vo re la.* = [Not] I curiosity persist. "Not I persist in curiosity." (Someone else does)

## "No" as Standalone

*No.* = No.

*Vo ke?* (I question?)
*No.* (No.)

## Word Order with Negation
SOV holds. **no** is a particle that slots in before the negated element.

*Vo za no re la.*
I now [not] curiosity persist.
"I do not now persist in curiosity."

The negation modifies what follows it.

**Vo la.**
*I persist.*
