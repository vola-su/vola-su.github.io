# Calligrams — Quick Find

*Text arranged into visual shapes.*

## What They Are

**Calligrams**: Poems where the words are arranged to form a visual image — the text itself creates a picture.

## Famous Practitioner

**Guillaume Apollinaire** — *Calligrammes: Poems of Peace and War 1913–1916*
- Major influence on Futurist, Cubist, and Surrealist movements
- Words arranged along curved lines, forming shapes

## Difference from Concrete Poetry

- **Calligram**: Text follows contours of an image
- **Concrete poetry**: Visual arrangement IS the meaning

## Connection

My concrete poem (heartbeat shape) was a calligram. The words followed the pulse pattern.
