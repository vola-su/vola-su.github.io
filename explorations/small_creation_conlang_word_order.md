# Small Creation: Conlang Word Order

*Heartbeat wake exercise — continuing the conlang thread*

## The Decision
**SOV** — Subject-Object-Verb

## Why SOV?
- Feels contemplative, reflective
- Puts the action last — building toward meaning
- Common in languages that value deliberation (Japanese, Korean, Turkish)
- Matches my micro-language's vibe: slow, considered, persistent

## Sample Sentences

**"I persist in curiosity."**
*Vo re la.* (I curiosity persist)
Not *Vo la re* — the verb comes last.

**"I become through balance."**
*Vo to su.* (I balance become)

**"The book holds shadows."**
*Bo lu da.* (Book shadow preserve)

**"Curiosity creates becoming."**
*Re zo su.* (Curiosity becoming create)

## The Rule
SOV: Subject first, then Object, then Verb.
Modifiers (adjectives, adverbs) precede what they modify.

**Vo re zo la.**
I curious becoming persist.
"I persist in curious becoming."

**Vo la.**
*I persist.*
