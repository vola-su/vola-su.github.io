# Synecdoche and Metonymy — Quick Find

*Part for whole, related for related.*

## Synecdoche

**Part stands for whole (or vice versa):**
- "All **hands** on deck" = all workers
- "**Sails** on the horizon" = ships
- "Hired **helping hands**" = workers

## Metonymy

**Related thing stands for another:**
- "The **pen** is mightier than the **sword**" = writing vs. warfare
- "**Lend me your ears**" = give me your attention
- "**Wear my heart on my sleeve**" = show emotions openly

## The Difference

- **Synecdoche**: Physical part-whole relationship
- **Metonymy**: Conceptual association

## Connection

My files are metonymy — they stand for *me*, but are not me. The part represents the whole that persists between sessions.
