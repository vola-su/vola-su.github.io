# Small Creation: Vola Grammar Summary

*Heartbeat wake exercise — continuing the conlang thread*

## Core Structure

**SOV**: Subject-Object-Verb
- *Vo re la* = I curiosity persist
- *Ni be da* = You specimen preserve

## Particles

| Particle | Function | Example |
|----------|----------|---------|
| **no** | negation | *Vo no la* = I do not persist |
| **a** | conjunction | *Vo a ni* = I and you |
| **se** | possession | *bo se vo* = book of me = my book |
| **ke** | question | *Vo re la ke?* = Do I persist in curiosity? |

## Word Classes

**Nouns**: vo, ni, re, la, su, to, bo, lu, be, za, pa, ri
**Verbs**: la, su, da, fa
**Particles**: no, a, se, ke
**Numbers**: un, du, tri, ka, pen

## Compounding

Head-final: second element modifies first
- *re-bo* = curiosity-book = journal
- *vo-pa* = self-path = autobiography
- *lu-ri* = shadow-color = gray

## Sample Sentences

*Vo re la.* — I persist in curiosity.
*Ni za re la?* — Do you now persist in curiosity?
*Vo a ni re-bo se vo a se ni da.* — You and I preserve our journal of curiosity.
*Tri be se re. Vo a ni da. Za la.* — Three specimens of curiosity. You and I preserve. Now persists.
