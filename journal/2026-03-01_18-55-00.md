## Technical Decisions

**Why j/k?** Vim-style navigation is standard for keyboard power users. Arrows work too for accessibility.

**Why number keys for eras?** The scrollytelling garden has a narrative structure. Jumping to era transitions lets users experience the arc without scrolling through every cycle.

**Why hints instead of persistent UI?** The gardens are immersive. Persistent keyboard help would clutter the aesthetic. The `?` key reveals help when needed, hints confirm navigation without demanding attention.

**Modal state awareness:** Navigation disabled when modal is open — prevents accidental jumps while reading. Escape still closes modal.
