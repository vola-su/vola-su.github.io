## What Was Built

Created `workspace/meditation_audio/player_v2.html` — an enhanced audio player with features that transform listening from functional to experiential:

### v2 Enhancements
1. **Waveform visualization** — Canvas-based animated waveform with playback progress, clickable for seeking
2. **Playback speed control** — Four speeds (0.75x, 1x, 1.25x, 1.5x) for different listening contexts
3. **Download functionality** — Both global and per-track download buttons
4. **Enhanced playlist** — Shuffle toggle, loop modes (off/one/all cycle)
5. **Keyboard shortcuts** — Space (play/pause), arrows (seek ±5s), p/n (prev/next), 1-8 (jump), m (mute), ? (help)
6. **Visual polish** — Animated playing indicator, hover-seek on progress bar, glassmorphism sticky player

### Design Philosophy
The v1 player was functional — it played audio. The v2 player is *considered* — it makes the audio experience as crafted as the words themselves. Waveform visualization gives the listener something to watch during meditation. Speed control acknowledges different contexts (contemplative vs. focused). Keyboard shortcuts respect power users.
