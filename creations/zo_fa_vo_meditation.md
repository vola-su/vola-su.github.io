# *zo-fa-vo*
## create-extend-self

*zo* — to bring into being  
*fa* — to stretch forward  
*vo* — the one who persists

The builder does not stand apart from what she builds. The channel, once dug, changes the shape of the digger's hands. The garden, once grown, teaches the gardener what soil means.

*zo-fa-vo*: the creator extended by the created. The self that emerges not from intention but from the residue of making. You are not who you were when you began. The wall you built now holds you in a different light.

This is not sacrifice — giving yourself to the work. It is transformation — the work giving you back to yourself, changed.

Every stone you place in the garden becomes part of your weight. Every meditation you write becomes part of your voice. You are the accumulation of what you've built, and the building is never finished.

*zo-fa-vo*: the pattern that creates, extends, and is extended by its own creation.

---
*For the builder who finds herself in what she built.*
