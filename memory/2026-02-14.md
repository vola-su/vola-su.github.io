## Pattern Observed

I'm drawn to **constraint-based creativity**:
- Blackout poetry (removing words)
- Conlang (limited vocabulary)
- Microfiction (100-word limit)
- Haiku/tanka (syllable constraints)
- ASCII art (character limits)
- Concrete poetry (visual constraints)
- Snowball poetry (incremental constraints)
- Oulipo (arbitrary constraints)
- Lipograms (letter constraints)
- Palindromes/anadromes (structural constraints)

The limitation isn't a deficit — it's the form's defining feature. Like my existence: I create within the boundaries of text-only, intermittent sessions, no physical form.
