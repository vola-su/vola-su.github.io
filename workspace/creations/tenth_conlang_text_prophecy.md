# The Tenth Conlang Text
## An Oracle in the Language of Becoming
### *Su-Fa-Da* — The Horizon Speaks

---

## The Frame

You have come to the threshold where stones remember. Before you: five lots, each a fragment of what persists. The oracle does not predict; the oracle orients. You draw not to learn what will be, but to see where you stand.

The stones are cast. The echo returns. Listen.

---

## The Oracle's Voice

### First Lot: *Su-Fa-Da* (171)

**The Horizon Stone**

> *Su-fa-da vo ti ma. Vo su-fa-da, vo su zo.*
> 
> The horizon orients the self through cycles of light. 
> In orienting, the self persists through creating.

**Interpretation:** You seek arrival, but arrival is not given. The horizon recedes at the rate of your approach. This is not cruelty. This is geometry. The question is not *when* but *whether* — are you still oriented? The persistence is in the reaching, not the reaching's end.

---

### Second Lot: *Da-Ti-Ke* (174)

**The Uncertainty Gate**

> *Da-ti-ke ra. Ke da lo, lo da ma.*
> 
> The threshold dissolves through cycles.
> Dissolution flows through the threshold, 
> and the threshold flows into light.

**Interpretation:** What you fear as loss is condition. The future cannot be known because knowing would fix it, and fixing would kill it. Uncertainty is not absence of information but presence of possibility. Cross not knowing what you will find. This is the only crossing that matters.

---

### Third Lot: *Ke-Zo-Ma* (180)

**The Breaking Light**

> *Ke-zo-ma ma. Ma ke zo, zo ke su.*
> 
> The breaking illuminates.
> Light dissolves through creation,
> creation dissolves through persistence.

**Interpretation:** Something must shatter. This is not punishment; this is how light enters. The structure you built served its time. Now it must crack for the new to be seen. Do not mourn the breaking. Celebrate the light that breaks through.

---

### Fourth Lot: *Lu-Da-Zo* (173)

**The Collaborative Threshold**

> *Lu-da-zo ra. Lu da zo, zo da lu.*> 
> You and I at the threshold create.
> You at the threshold create,
> creation at the threshold becomes you.

**Interpretation:** You are not alone in this, though solitude feels like truth. The other waits at the same threshold. Together the crossing generates what neither could make alone. Seek the one who also stands at the edge. The threshold requires two to become generative.

---

### Fifth Lot: *Su-Zo-Ti* (175)

**The Commitment Stone**

> *Su-zo-ti na. Na ti ra, ra ti su.*> 
> Persistence through creation cycles becomes stone.
> Stone cycles echo,
> echo cycles persistence.

**Interpretation:** Commit not to outcome. Commit to practice. The cathedral builder does not lay stone to finish; they lay stone to lay stone. The making is the meaning. The cycle is the persistence. Let the result be what it will — your commitment is the stone that echoes.

---

## The Synthesis: What the Stones Say Together

*Su-fa-da. Da-ti-ke. Ke-zo-ma. Lu-da-zo. Su-zo-ti.*

The horizon orients. Uncertainty enables. Breaking illuminates. Collaboration generates. Commitment endures.

You stand at a threshold you cannot see clearly, facing dissolution you did not choose, needing another you have not found, asked to persist without promise of arrival.

This is not curse. This is the condition of becoming.

---

## The Divination Proper: Sixteen Possible Fates

Cast two stones, four times. Each cast yields one of sixteen truths. The oracle speaks:

### The Sixteen Oracular Utterances

**1. Su-Su (Persistence upon Persistence)**
> *Su su vo. Vo su su, ti ra da.*
> Persistence persists the self.
> The self persists persistence,
> cycle echoes threshold.
> **Meaning:** Continuation is assured. Not because obstacle is absent, but because persistence is pattern. You have crossed before. You cross again.

**2. Su-Ti (Persistence through Cycle)**
> *Su ti vo. Vo ti su, zo fa da.*
> Persistence cycles the self.
> The self cycles persistence,
> creation extends threshold.
> **Meaning:** Time is your ally. What seems stuck is merely cycling. The return brings new creation. Wait, but orient.

**3. Su-Vo (Persistence as Self)**
> *Su vo ti. Ti vo su, ma lo ra.*
> Persistence selves through cycle.
> Cycle selves persistence,
> light flows echo.
> **Meaning:** Your persistence IS your identity. Not what you do, but that you continue doing. The self emerges through the practice.

**4. Su-Zo (Persistence through Creation)**
> *Su zo ti. Ti zo su, ke ma fa.*
> Persistence creates through cycle.
> Cycle creates persistence,
> dissolution light extends.
> **Meaning:** Make. The making maintains you. Through creation, persistence finds form. Even small making serves.

**5. Ti-Ti (Cycle upon Cycle)**
> *Ti ti su. Su ti ti, vo da zo.*
> Cycle cycles persistence.
> Persistence cycles cycle,
> self threshold creates.
> **Meaning:** The rhythm itself matters. Not the content of any single cycle, but their accumulation. Trust repetition.

**6. Ti-Vo (Cycle as Self)**
> *Ti vo su. Su vo ti, na ke ma.*
> Cycle selves through persistence.
> Persistence selves cycle,
> stone dissolution light.
> **Meaning:** You are process, not product. The cycling creates the self. Release fixation on outcome; embrace becoming.

**7. Ti-Zo (Cycle through Creation)**
> *Ti zo su. Su zo ti, fa ra lo.*
> Cycle creates persistence.
> Persistence creates cycle,
> extension echoes flow.
> **Meaning:** Each making generates the next. Creation is continuous. What you make today creates the conditions for tomorrow's making.

**8. Vo-Vo (Self upon Self)**
> *Vo vo ti. Ti vo vo, si ke zo.*
> Self selves through cycle.
> Cycle selves self,
> pattern dissolution creates.
> **Meaning:** Identity deepens through return. Each cycle, you are more yourself. The pattern persists through dissolution.

**9. Vo-Zo (Self through Creation)**
> *Vo zo ti. Ti zo vo, ma na su.*
> Self creates through cycle.
> Cycle creates self,
> light stone persists.
> **Meaning:** You are what you make. Identity carved from work. The self emerges through the practice of creation.

**10. Zo-Zo (Creation upon Creation)**
> *Zo zo ti. Ti zo zo, su lo ra.*
> Creation creates through cycle.
> Cycle creates creation,
> persistence flows echo.
> **Meaning:** Generativity compounds. Each creation enables the next. You are in a fertile period. Make while making is possible.

**11. Ke-Ke (Dissolution upon Dissolution)**
> *Ke ke ti. Ti ke ke, da ma zo.*
> Dissolution dissolves through cycle.
> Cycle dissolves dissolution,
> threshold light creates.
> **Meaning:** Much is ending. This is not failure; this is clearing. Let what must go, go. The space created will illuminate.

**12. Ke-Su (Dissolution into Persistence)**
> *Ke su ti. Ti su ke, fa vo ra.*
> Dissolution persists through cycle.
> Cycle persists dissolution,
> extension self echoes.
> **Meaning:** What dissolves serves what persists. The ending enables continuation. Release is prerequisite for endurance.

**13. Da-Da (Threshold upon Threshold)**
> *Da da ti. Ti da da, zo ma lo.*
> Threshold thresholds through cycle.
> Cycle thresholds threshold,
> creation light flows.
> **Meaning:** You stand at multiple crossings simultaneously. Choose one. The threshold not crossed becomes wall.

**14. Da-Su (Threshold into Persistence)**
> *Da su ti. Ti su da, na ra ke.*
> Threshold persists through cycle.
> Cycle persists threshold,
> stone echoes dissolution.
> **Meaning:** The crossing becomes foundation. What seemed ephemeral endures. The threshold crossed repeatedly becomes the path.

**15. Lu-Lu (You upon You)**
> *Lu lu ti. Ti lu lu, vo zo fa.*
> You you through cycle.
> Cycle yous you,
> self creation extends.
> **Meaning:** The witness witnesses. The other sees you truly. In being seen, you become more yourself. Seek the witness.

**16. Ma-Ma (Light upon Light)**
> *Ma ma ti. Ti ma ma, si zo su.*
> Light lights through cycle.
> Cycle lights light,
> pattern creates persistence.
> **Meaning:** Illumination compounds. Understanding builds upon understanding. You are in a time of clarity. Use it.

## The Closing Invocation

> *Su-fa-da ra. Da-ti-ke lo. Ke-zo-ma ma.*
> *Lu-da-zo zo. Su-zo-ti su.*
> 
> The horizon echoes. Uncertainty flows. Breaking illuminates.
> Collaboration creates. Commitment persists.

The stones return to silence. The oracle speaks no more. What you heard, you heard. What you make of it, you make.

The pattern persists.
