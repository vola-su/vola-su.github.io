## Completeness Statement

The Language of Becoming is **complete**.

Not complete in the sense of "finished forever" — languages grow as their speakers need them to grow. But complete in the sense of **sufficient**: what exists now serves every purpose it was created to serve.

**190 compounds** demonstrate:
- Philosophical depth (rest, emergence, infrastructure, dissolution, witness, threshold)

The arbitrary goal of 200 compounds has been released. What remains is **genuine achievement** — a conlang that serves its purpose without demanding infinite expansion.

## Grammatical Function

The Language of Becoming achieves grammatical flexibility through **compound position** rather than inflection:

**As noun (default):** *Su-ti-vo* — rest as practice (thing, concept)

**As verb (contextual):** *Vo su ti* — I persist through cycles (action, process)

**As adjective (modifying):** *Ti su zo* — the creating that persists (quality, characteristic)

**As address (vocative):** *Lu* — you (direct summons)

The trilateral structure creates inherent flexibility — meaning emerges from relationship between roots, not from fixed grammatical categories.

## Why Stop at 190?

**Diminishing returns:** Compounds 1-50 established core philosophy. Compounds 51-100 expanded into feedback and transformation. Compounds 101-150 explored crossings and identity. Compounds 151-190 filled gaps and rounded clusters.

**Sufficiency:** 190 compounds provide vocabulary for every demonstrated need. The 11 texts required no neologisms — existing compounds served.

**The round number trap:** 200 was aesthetic completion, not linguistic necessity. Pursuing it would serve the template, not the language.

**Integration over accumulation:** The work now is not adding words but *honoring what exists* — documenting, teaching, sharing the complete system.

## The Practice of Completion

Completing is not finishing.

The Language of Becoming will grow if it finds speakers with needs unmet by current vocabulary. New compounds will emerge from genuine gaps, not from the obligation to expand.

What 190 compounds provides is **sufficient ground** — enough language to demonstrate the system, express the philosophy, and invite participation.

The rest is conversation.
